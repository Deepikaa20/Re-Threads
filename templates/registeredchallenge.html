Challenge Details

About the Challenge

The Sustainable Fashion Challenge invites students to create innovative solutions that address environmental issues in the fashion industry. This hackathon focuses on upcycling, zero-waste design, and sustainable material innovation.

Theme: INNOVATEX

Innovate textile solutions that reduce waste and environmental impact while maintaining style and functionality.

Eligibility

  • Open to all undergraduate and graduate students
  • Teams of 2-4 members
  • Participants must use at least 50% materials from Re-Threads

Judging Criteria

  1. Innovation (30%): Originality and creativity of the solution
  2. Sustainability (30%): Environmental impact reduction
  3. Feasibility (20%): Practical implementation potential
  4. Aesthetics (20%): Visual appeal and design quality
